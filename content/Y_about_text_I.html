Blind Monkey — Full Bio

Blind Monkey is a five-piece rock band from Antwerp that lives for the stage. Their sound digs deep into hard rock with a grunge edge and an ethnic soul — somewhere between Alice in Chains, Led Zeppelin and Tool, but woven through with dark, primitive sounds from outside the Western scale.

Politically charged lyrics, raw energy, and something you won't easily forget. The band tackles contemporary issues through sharp, occasionally sardonic commentary, delivered with the kind of intensity that makes walls shake and ears ring.

The lineup brings together Imke on lead vocals, Barak on guitar and vocals, Bart on guitar, Faisal on bass, and Alexis on drums. Their diverse musical backgrounds converge into a sound that feels both primal and urgent — heavy riffs grounded in groove, vocals that cut through the noise, and a rhythm section that hits like a freight train.

With three original tracks already out on Spotify — Run Man, Wake Up! and Hanxiety — Blind Monkey is building momentum fast. They play tight, energetic sets with no complicated demands. Support slot, headliner, last-minute fill-in — they're ready to go.

If you want a night full of riffs, raw energy, and pure passion: Blind Monkey delivers.
